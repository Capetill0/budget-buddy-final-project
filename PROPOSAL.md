# Final Project Proposal 
- I am naming this poject/app Budget Buddy 

# What I'm building
- Budget Buddy is meant to be a personal finance dashboard that helps people track expenses, manage savings goals, and understand their overall financial health.

# Why I'm building it
- I find personal finance very important and I feel like many people struggle to organize their finances and savings in one place, which affects the amount of people actually tracing their budgets or expenses. This tool would be able to provide a simple way to track spending and savings progress.

# App Features
- Dashboard with financial summary
- Expense tracker with categories
- Savings goal tracker with progress bars
- Responsive multi-section layout/navigation

# Stretch Goals
- Spending charts/graphs
- Dark mode
- Monthly budget breakdowns
